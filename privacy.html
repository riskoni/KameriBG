Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 23, 2025

Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application Kameri BG handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

Kameri BG does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No user information is collected or tracked by the app or the developer.

2. Location Access

The app uses your device's GPS functionality to determine your current speed zone. This data is used only on your device and is not transmitted, stored, or shared with anyone.

3. No Internet Connection

Kameri BG operates entirely offline. The app does not connect to the internet for any reason. This ensures that all your data remains private and local to your device.

4. Data Sharing

Since Kameri BG does not collect or store any data, there is nothing to share with third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

Kameri BG does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. However, given that the app does not collect data, substantial changes are unlikely. Any updates will be posted on this page.

7. Contact
